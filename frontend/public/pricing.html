Frequently asked questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes! Every plan comes with a 14-day free pilot. No credit card required, and you can cancel anytime.

How long does setup take?

Most teams are up and running in under 30 minutes. Our onboarding team will help configure your knowledge base and channels.

How do I update the knowledge base?

Managers can upload, edit, and remove documents from the control centre at any time. Changes are reflected instantly for all reps.

How is my data secured?

We use enterprise-grade encryption at rest and in transit, SOC 2 compliance, and offer SSO/SAML on Enterprise plans.

Do my reps need to install anything?

No. Salesji works through WhatsApp, Telegram, and a web app — no downloads or installations needed.

What if I need more reps than my plan allows?

You can upgrade your plan at any time, or contact us for custom Enterprise pricing to fit your team size.
